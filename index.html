  1. Project Description: Summarize the project as clearly as one can.
  2. Project Purpose: Who benefits and why. What are the justifications for it?
  3. Project Objectives: More concrete. Unpack the project into statements.
  4. Stakeholders: Client, other students, maybe even us.
  5. Requirements: What are they for this to be successful?
  6. Deliverables and acceptance criteria: What is needed to get this done? What are the minimum requirements (maybe some numbers or something)?
  7. Assumptions: The factors that are in place for the project to work. (f.ex, time, team composition, and reliability).
  8. Constraints: Time, cost, and the scope of the project.
  9. Risks: What will impact the project negatively? Classify the dangers and how impactful they might be.
  10. Milestones: Work: The actual deliverables to get the project done. Management: Generic stuff, plan, find material, etc.
